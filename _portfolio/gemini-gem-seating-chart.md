## Overview
This Gemini Gem generates classroom seating charts based on structured input such as class size, student constraints, and instructional priorities.

It is designed to reduce setup time and help teachers quickly iterate on seating arrangements that support classroom management and learning goals.

## Key Capabilities
- Accepts class size and room constraints
- Supports behavioral or instructional grouping considerations
- Produces clear, printable seating layouts

## Notes
This tool is part of an ongoing series of Gemini Gems focused on classroom workflow optimization.
